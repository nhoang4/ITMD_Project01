Black Myth: Wukong

Overview

Black Myth: Wukong is an action RPG by Game Science Studio. The game is based on the famous Chinese novel, Journey to the West, and features Sun Wukong, the legendary Monkey King. Players will get to experience a highly detailed world filled with intense combat, magical transformations, and breathtaking visuals.

Gameplay

The game offers fast-paced, challenging combat reminiscent of Souls-like titles. Bosses can be fought using a variety of weapons and spells while transforming into different creatures to gain an edge in battle.

Graphics

Developed using Unreal Engine 5, the game features stunning visuals with realistic environments, detailed character models, and advanced lighting effects. The use of ray tracing adds a layer of visual fidelity that sets it apart from other games.

Available Platforms

It is released on PC and popular consoles, including PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S.
